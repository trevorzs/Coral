Coral

Welcome to Coral, a web app created by Trevor Zachary Smith.

All corals are procedurally generated.

Hover over any coral to highlight it

Each coral possesses unique traits that can be passed on to the next generation

Click on a coral to generate a new set of coral based on the clicked coral's traits

The sliders on the top right control structural mutation rate and color mutation rate

These mutation rates dictate the chance for a coral to have a drastically different color/shape

If you don't set a number on the slider, the corals will come with inherent mutation rates. (They are usually quite low)

Have fun evolving coral to your aesthetic desires!
